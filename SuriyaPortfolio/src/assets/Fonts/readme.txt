Glonto is a clean and modern sans serif font designed with simplicity and elegance in mind. Perfectly suited for branding, UI/UX design, editorial projects, and creative work, Glonto offers a professional and fresh look that makes your designs stand out. With its balanced proportions and crisp lines, Glonto ensures excellent readability and a contemporary aesthetic. This versatile typeface works great for logos, headlines, posters, digital and print designs.
